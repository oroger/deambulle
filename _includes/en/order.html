How to place an order?

An order at Deambulle's consists of 4 steps:

First contact

You fill in the form below by listing the items that interest you and the desired amount.

We call you back !

We will contact you to finalize the offer, set a payment method and agree on a delivery method.

Payment

You choose between three payment methods: PayPal, bank transfer or cash. The shipping costs are your responsibility.

Delivery

You can come in person in order to retrieve your parcel or we will ship it to you. You can finally enjoy it.
