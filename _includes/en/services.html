Wooden

Most of our games are made of rubber wood. Rubber plantations are sustainable and respectful of the environment. Some pieces come from Jura (France).

Handmade

Our games are handcrafted with the assistance of human-sized equipments and painted by hands with colours composed of ecological solvents.

For everyone

Rules are simple and fit on one page. They are available in several languages and offer many variants which allows for endless fun.

All-in-one

The game, the gameboard and the box of most of our games are the same objects for easy transport and storage.

Warranty

We'll make our best to provide you with any game parts you might have lost.

Timeless

Beautiful, sustainable, resistant, warm... our wooden games will never get old.
